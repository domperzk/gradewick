# Gradewick (Warwick Grade Tracker)

A sleek, local-first academic dashboard for tracking grades, calculating degree averages, and predicting module targets at the University of Warwick.

## Core Philosophy
- **100% Private:** Your data is saved *only* in your browser's `localStorage`. There is no backend, no database, and your academic data never leaves your device.
- **Warwick Integrated:** Powered by a deep-scraped database of over 3,500+ Warwick University modules. It auto-fills CATS credits, assessment components, and exact weightings the moment you type a module code.
- **Predictive Math:** Automatically handles CATS weightings across multiple years and calculates exactly what marks you need in future, ungraded assignments to reach your target degree classification.

## Features
- **Smart Dashboard:** Visual overview of your academic progress, upcoming deadlines, and current degree boundary.
- **Course Onboarding:** Quick-start by selecting your department and degree to automatically load your core module structure.
- **Target Grade Planner:** A dynamic "what-if" calculator that lets you simulate future marks and shows exactly what you need to achieve a First, 2:1, etc.
- **Unified Timetable:** Filter upcoming assessments by category (Exam, Coursework, etc.) or completion status.
- **Revision Checklist:** Track study progress for every module topic with interactive progress bars.
- **Complete Customization:** Full Dark Mode support, compact layout toggles, and customizable UI accent colors.
- **Data Portability:** Download your grades as a CSV spreadsheet or backup/restore your entire profile via JSON. 

## Usage
No installation, account creation, or setup required. Since it is a completely static web app, you can run it instantly by opening `index.html` in any browser, or by visiting the live link:

**[https://gradewick.com](https://gradewick.com)**
